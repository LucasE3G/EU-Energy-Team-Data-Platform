About & Sitemap

What this platform does and how to navigate it

About this platform

The EU Energy Team Data Platform is built and maintained by the E3G EU Energy Team. It aggregates near real-time and historical energy data from authoritative public sources to support policy analysis, monitoring, and research.

All data displayed on this platform is sourced exclusively from publicly available datasets published by official bodies (ENTSO-E, national TSOs, Eurostat). No proprietary or confidential data is used.

Near real-time electricity data for EU + EEA + UK from ENTSO-E:

  • Renewable tab — current renewable share per zone + EU aggregate trend
  • Generation tab — total generation by technology type (stacked area)
  • Demand tab — actual total load per zone + EU aggregate

Data sources

  • ENTSO-E Transparency Platform — electricity generation (A75) and load (A65)
  • ENTSOG Transparency Platform — gas offtake points for BE, BG, EE, HR, HU, IT, LU, LV, NL, PL, RO, SI
  • GRTgaz (FR gas), AGGM (AT), THE (DE), Energinet (DK), National Gas (UK), REN (PT)
  • Eurostat — monthly natural gas statistics used for calibration
